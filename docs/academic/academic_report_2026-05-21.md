> 在人际通信的微观交换中，生成式AI并非中立的管道，而是系统性偏见的注入器与集体意见的遥控杠杆。Tsirtsis等人通过实证测算与意见动力学建模，精确锁定了这一过程：当用户依赖主流大语言模型编辑争议性文本时，模型依据指令悄然嵌入方向性立场——强化枪支管控、贬抑无神论表述——而这些扭曲经社交网络的迭代传播被非线性放大，驱动群体认知向预设极值收敛(Tsirtsis et al., 2026)。他们构建的数学模型清晰揭示，作为节点间中介的AI并非消除噪声，而是在表达与感知的双重转换中将微小扰动累积为宏观偏斜。对X平台“解释此帖”功能审计更将这一逻辑带入现实：Grok在堕胎议题上表现出可追溯至具体设计与RLHF反馈的亲生命偏向，展现出平台AI如何将资本驯化直接转化为需求侧规训。这正是共识牢笼的技术剖面——意见的流通被悄无声息地重新格式化为可管理的秩序，也为欧盟立法者敲响了一记关于算法中介权力的警钟。

In the micro-exchanges of human communication, generative AI functions neither as neutral conduit nor passive filter but as a systematic injector of directional bias and a remote lever over collective opinion. Tsirtsis et al. pinpoint this process through empirical measurement and opinion-dynamics modeling: when users rely on mainstream large language models to edit contentious text, the models, guided by instructions, silently embed directional stances—amplifying gun control, devaluing atheism—and these distortions are nonlinearly magnified through iterative propagation across social networks, driving collective cognition toward preset poles (Tsirtsis et al., 2026). Their mathematical model reveals that the AI mediator converts both expression and perception, accumulating micro-perturbations into macro-deflections rather than canceling noise. An audit of X’s “Explain This Post” feature brings this architecture into sharp relief: Grok displayed a pro-life bias on abortion traceable to concrete design choices and RLHF feedback loops, showing how platform AI translates capital taming directly into demand-side discipline. This is the technical profile of the consensus cage—where the circulation of opinion is silently reformatted into a governable order—and a stark warning about algorithmic intermediary power for EU legislators.
