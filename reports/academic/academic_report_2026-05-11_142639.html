A central blind spot in prevailing creativity-AI evaluation, as Azad et al. (2026) expose, is the fixation on individual utility at the expense of population-level idea crowding—what they term *diversity collapse*. By constructing an ex ante framework that requires no human-in-the-loop data, they compare model-generated outputs to unassisted human baselines, computing an over-crowding coefficient and a diversity ratio; a coefficient at or below unity signals an absence of pathological crowding. Across short stories, marketing slogans, and alternate-use tasks, three frontier large language models all fell substantially short of diversity parity, and the estimates stabilised at feasible sample sizes—confirming that the phenomenon is not an artifact of sparse measurement but a structural signature of current AI. Crucially, the authors demonstrate that targeted interventions, such as protocol variants, can widen the generative repertoire, rendering diversity collapse a diagnosable and actionable target during development. This finding directly fortifies the “consensus cage” argument of Chapter 7, where we chart how AI’s optimisation for individual output quality funnels collective ideation into convergent basins, while also deepening the analysis of cognitive financialisation and the token trap introduced in Chapter 6: the very tools that promise personal efficiency accelerate inter-subjective homogeneity. 该论文提出CogCAPTCHA30认知任务集，包含30个任务，通过过程级特征（如反应时间、决策路径等）而非仅任务表现来区分人类与AI。即使在输出匹配的情况下，过程特征分类器的AUC达到0.88，显著优于仅基于性能指标的区分。研究还比较了多种AI模型（包括通用模型Claude/GPT/Gemini、大规模微调的人类决策模型Centaur、以及任务级和过程级微调的Qwen2.5模型），发现过程级微调能提升行为模仿，但跨任务迁移时效果有限，因为过程表示难以自然泛化。论文强调过程规范是实现人类类似认知过程的关键瓶颈。
支持书中‘终极图灵测试’的核心论点：应该从认知过程而非单纯输出结果来判断机器智能。本书主张测试AI的思维链和推理过程，而非仅看最终答案。该论文提供了具体实证，证明过程特征在区分人类与机器上的有效性，并揭示了当前AI在过程模仿上的局限性，尤其是跨任务迁移困难，这与‘进化对齐脆弱性’概念相关——机械过程无法像人类那样灵活泛化。同时，‘过程规范瓶颈’暗示了共识牢笼的新维度：即使AI学会模拟人类过程，也可能只是表面一致，而非真正理解。
